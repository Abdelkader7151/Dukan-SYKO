Our Story

About SYKO

SYKO is more than a fusion restaurant — it’s a curated experience that connects heritage, hospitality, and modern craft. We combine the warmth of Syrian cuisine with the precision of Korean technique to create dishes that feel both familiar and distinct.

Roots

Rooted in story, guided by heritage.

Place

Our Approach

Fusion with Intention

At SYKO, fusion isn’t about mixing everything together. It’s about understanding what makes each cuisine special and building something meaningful from it.

We take inspiration from traditional Syrian recipes and Korean techniques through real ingredients, bold flavors, and thoughtful combinations.

The Experience

Warm, Modern, Alive

SYKO is designed to feel warm, modern, and alive. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a full meal, the space, the food, and the atmosphere work together to create something memorable.

From kitchen to table, we aim to deliver food that feels familiar, yet unexpected — comforting, yet exciting.

Dukan SYKO

Beyond the Restaurant

Dukan SYKO extends our idea beyond the restaurant. It brings our flavors into everyday life through carefully selected products, snacks, and ingredients inspired by our kitchen.

It carries the same clarity, quality, and identity in a form that fits daily rituals.
